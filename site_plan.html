Site Name

michaelson Chamber of Commerce. This name was selected because it clearly represents the chamber of commerce serving michaelson.

Site Purpose

The website aims to promote local and international businesses and services, provide community material and resources, and schedule events to foster economic growth.

Scenarios

  • How can I join the chamber as a member?
  • What networking events are scheduled for this month?
  • Who are the current board members?

Color Schema

Primary color: Blue (#0000FF) for headers and navigation.

Secondary color: Light gray (#F0F0F0) for background.

Typography

"Open Sans" for body text.

"Roboto" for headings.
